# Reference: Levels

## [Level 1] Brink Valley
*The Safe Zone Hub*
- **Lore:** A peaceful valley protected from the Aether Storm.
- **Function:** Trade, Social, Quest Hub.
- **PvP:** Disabled.

## [Level 2] Emerald Labyrinth (Forest)
*Simulated Forest / Starter Zone*
- **Lvl Range:** 1 - 15
- **Boss:** Forest Warden
- **Main Quests:** 15-20 narrative steps leading to the Boss.

## [Level 3] Crypt of the Damned
*Dark Dungeon / Solo Leveling Style*
- **Atmosphere:** Pitch black, torches or light spells required.
- **Mechanics:** Hidden traps, wave-based undead combat.
